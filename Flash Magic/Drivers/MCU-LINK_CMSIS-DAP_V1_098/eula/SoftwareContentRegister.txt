This is the "eula" subdirectory of the top-level directory
for the NXP MCU-Link Probe Firmware package.

The NXP MCU-Link CMSIS-DAP Probe Firmware is owned by NXP B.V.

As detailed in the Software Content Register below, the NXP MCU-Link CMSIS-DAP
Probe Firmware package comprises NXP copyrighted files, MCU-Link probe firmware
and host specific scripts, utilities and drivers.
NXP copyrighted files are licensed under the terms of the NXP B.V. proprietary
license associated with this distribution (see LA_OPT_NXP_Software_License.txt).

The MCU-Link probe firmware image is delivered in .s19 format.
Portions of MCUXpresso SDK LPC55S69 have been used to build the firmware.
The package also includes host specific scripts, utilities, drivers.
